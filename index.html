Exam Strategy

How to approach any physics problem

STEP 1
Draw a diagram. Sketch the system, label all known quantities.
STEP 2
Choose a principle. Newton's laws? Energy conservation? Momentum?
STEP 3
Write the equation. State it symbolically before plugging numbers.
STEP 4
Check units. Your answer must have the right dimensions.
