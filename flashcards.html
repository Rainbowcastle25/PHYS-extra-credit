Active Recall Tips

TIP 01

Say the answer out loud before flipping — this forces retrieval, not just recognition.

TIP 02

Do 2–3 rounds: first pass all cards, second pass only Review cards, third pass timed.

TIP 03

Shuffle the deck on every new session to avoid memorizing card order instead of content.
